# [3260] 두 수의 덧셈
> [SW Expert Academy](https://swexpertacademy.com/main/main.do){: target="_blank"}

## my solution
```python
for t in range(int(input())):
    A, B = map(int, input().split())
    print('#{0} {1}'.format(t+1, A + B))
```
